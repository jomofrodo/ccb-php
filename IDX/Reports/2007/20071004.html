Prayer of Peace: Relief & Resistance in Burma's War Zones

30 minutes - High Definition - On the front line of conflict deep inside Burma this documentary follows ethnic relief workers as they aid internally displaced people suffering under the Burma Army. Focusing on a female medic and a pastor/human rights cameraman, the film reveals a people that have maintained their dignity and hope for peace despite the odds.

The new film "Prayer of Peace: Relief & Resistance in Burma's War Zones" will screen Tuesday October 2nd at 7:30 pm at the Alliance Francaise in Chiang Mai, Thailand on 138 Charoen Prathet Road.

There will also be a screening in Bangkok, Tuesday October 9th at 8pm at the Foreign Correspondents Club of Thailand. The FCCT is located on the Penthouse floor of the Maneeya Center, 518/5 Ploenchit Road.

Thank you to everyone who is helping to make these things happen. I sincerely appreciate your help. And I truly hope through our efforts we are helping our friends in Burma in some way."
