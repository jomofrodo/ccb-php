Christ Calling the Talako

A new film documents a 50 year mission to the Talako people on the Thai-Burma border.

 

Raised in Texas, Allan Eubank has been a missionary in Thailand since 1961. This short documentary is the story of his work with the Talako people. A story of faith, perseverance and victory in Jesus Christ.
