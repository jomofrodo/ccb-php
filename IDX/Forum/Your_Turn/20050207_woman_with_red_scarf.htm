Woman With A Red Scarf

She is born to an innocent woman
Who happens to be an ethnic Karen.
Her parents work as hard as ants,
to bring their only child to Kawthoolei,
Their ancestral land 
Kawthoolei is a heritage of the Karen
Which is inside Burma
Karen’s capital state of Pa-an
Is ruled by dictator junta
The land which everyone wants
The daughter of the Karen woman
speaks in Karen dialect which makes the Burmese dictator mad
When she performs a Karen traditional dance
She wears a white dress and a white scarf on her head
To show that she is pure and innocent
Day in and out the invader dictator
Comes in to conquer her land
He burns the children in midday sun
And destroys the father and the son
She hops as fast as a calf with her head band white scarf
He seizes her on the shoulder
And drags her down on the floor
She begs her predator with all her heart
But he makes her dress turns to dark
He strikes her throat apart and wraps it with her white scarf
She lies motionless with her neck scar.
