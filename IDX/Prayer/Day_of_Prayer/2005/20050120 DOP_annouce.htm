March 13, 2005 Global Day of Prayer for Burma

January 20, 2005

Dear Friends,

Thank you for continuing to keep Burma in your prayers. By praying with those around the world, including those displaced from their homes inside Burma, you can be a part of God's solution for this country that has struggled through civil war and dictatorship for over half a century.

March 13, 2005  is the upcoming date for the GLOBAL DAY OF PRAYER FOR BURMA.
We hope that this date will be marked on your calendars and that all of you can join the people of Burma in asking for God's will to be done in their country.

A prayer booklet has been mailed to everyone on our mailing list and videos about the situation of the Internally Displaced Persons (IDPs) of Burma are available. The prayer booklet in PDF form will also be on our website, www.prayforburma.org.

Thank you and God Bless you,

Christians Concerned for Burma

*** March 12, 2006 is next year's date for the GLOBAL DAY OF PRAYER FOR BURMA***
