Prayer of Peace: Relief & Resistance in Burma's War Zones

30 minutes - High Definition - On the front line of conflict deep inside Burma this documentary follows ethnic relief workers as they aid internally displaced people suffering under the Burma Army. Focusing on a female medic and a pastor/human rights cameraman, the film reveals a people that have maintained their dignity and hope for peace despite the odds.

The new film "Prayer of Peace: Relief & Resistance in Burma's War Zones" will screen Tuesday October 2nd at 7:30 pm at the Alliance Francaise in Chiang Mai, Thailand on 138 Charoen Prathet Road.

There will also be a screening in Bangkok, Tuesday October 9th at 8pm at the Foreign Correspondents Club of Thailand. The FCCT is located on the Penthouse floor of the Maneeya Center, 518/5 Ploenchit Road.

From the filmmaker:

"At both screenings there will be a discussion afterwards with me, a Free Burma Rangers relief team member, and someone from the Thai community. Thank you to everyone who is helping to make these things happen. I sincerely appreciate your help. And I truly hope through our efforts we are helping our friends in Burma in some way."

DVDs will be available from Silkworm Books.

About CCB

CCB works to support the people of Burma through prayer, evangelism, advocacy and action. CCB's belief is that God's love is alive in Burma and its light will not be put out. Though the people are suffering under an oppressive dictatorship, they have not given up. We have experienced the love and redemption of Jesus Christ and want to share this as the Lord leads and opens hearts.

Each year the CCB coordinates the Global Day of Prayer for Burma. On the second Sunday of March, CCB invites people around the world to pray and take action for the people of Burma. Throughout the year the CCB produces newsletters and information updates on the situation in Burma. For the Global Day of Prayer for Burma, CCB coordinates global prayer and advocacy activities as well as providing resources for those who participate. Holistic support in both Thailand and Burma are coordinated through CCB. CCB website: www.prayforburma.org
