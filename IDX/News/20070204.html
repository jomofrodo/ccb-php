80 Hours of Prayer for Burma

Psalm 70-
For the director of music. Of David. A Petition.

Hasten, O God, to save me. O Lord, Come quickly to help me. May those who
seek my life be put to shame and confusion; may all who desire my ruin be
turned back in disgrace. May those who say to me, 'Aha! Aha!' turn back
because of their shame. But may all who seek you rejoice and be glad in you;
may those who love your salvation always say, 'Let God be exalted!' Yet I
am poor and needy; come quickly to me, O God. You are my help and my
deliverer; O Lord do not delay.

Christians Concerned for Burma along with Partners Relief & Development is
hosting an event called "³80 Hours of Prayer for Burma" to take place in an
on-site prayer room in Chiang Mai beginning at noon on Thursday, February
15th through till Sunday, February 18th, 2007, at which time we will close
the event with a gathering in the prayer room from 6-8pm to pray and worship
together.

For all those suffering in Burma, our intention is to commit these 80 Hours
to coming before God in petition, asking him to hasten the end of the war
that has divided Burma for so long.
Those in Chiang Mai are encouraged to sign up for an hour slot in the prayer
room. While for others who would like to join with us in prayer from
wherever they are, please still sign up and just let us know where you will
be praying from.
