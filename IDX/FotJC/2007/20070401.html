Dear friends,

We spent most of the past year in the areas under attack by the Burma Army. Over 25,000 people are newly displaced and attacks continue. Thanks to your help, we now have 29 fulltime relief teams bringing emergency help and love to people in the war zones of Burma. They shine a light on the actions of the dictators’ army and help to build up ethnic leaders united to serve their people. Thanks for being with us in this.

Through all of this we are sustained by your love, support and prayers. We are also sustained by God’s word and one line in Psalm 23 has been especially meaningful to us. We hope it is useful to you:

“He prepares a table for me in the presence of my enemies”

We want to enjoy times in life without enemies or those who misunderstand us but that is often not the reality. We are faced with the choice of whether we will appreciate what God has for us and focus on that, or always be looking over our shoulder. Being with the people who are under attack, living a full life with our team members, thanking God for the things we have and can do is a gift and a challenge.

May God give us all the faith we need to appreciate His gifts and to live with joy and freedom.

God bless you,

Dave, Karen, Sahale, Suuzanne, Peter Eliya Eubank and all the team here.
